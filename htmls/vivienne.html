Giant Destroyer of All Things
Ultimate

Summons a storm, dealing damage equal to 260% of ATK to the nearest enemy and 4m around the enemy, and then launches the enemy into the air. Also deals additional damage equal to 90% of ATK as many as the number of "Wind Blade" buffs.

Upgrade 1:

Damage changes 280%. Additional damage changes to 95%.

Upgrade 2:

Damage changes 300%. Additional damage changes to 100%.

Cutting Wind Blade
Main

Create and throw a blade of wind. Deals damage equal to 180% of ATK in a 1.5m wide straight line to the nearest enemy and obtains the "Wind Blade" buff which increases Crit Rate by 5% (up to 3 stacks). Also deals additional damage equal to 90% of ATK as many as the number of Wind Blade buffs.

Upgrade 1:

Damage changes to 190%, additional damage changes to 95%.

Upgrade 2:

Damage changes to 200%, additional damage changes to 100%. Max number of "Wind Blade" stacks changes to 4 stacks.
